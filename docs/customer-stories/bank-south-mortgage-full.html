About the Company

BankSouth Mortgage

BankSouth Mortgage is a wholly-owned subsidiary of BankSouth, a leading community bank headquartered at Lake Oconee in Greensboro, Georgia. Recognized as a top-performing bank in the country with over $1.3 billion in assets, BankSouth has built a strong reputation for personalized service and local expertise.

BankSouth Mortgage originates over $2 billion annually in home loans through its team of approximately 60 loan officers, serving customers across the Southeast and throughout the United States. The mortgage division leverages nCino as its point-of-sale platform—where roughly 85% of applications originate—and Encompass as its loan origination system.

The $400K verification problem.

Before partnering with Truv, BankSouth Mortgage was spending approximately $400,000 annually on income and employment verifications through multiple traditional providers. Individual loan verification costs were reaching $200+ per file on 40% of loans, with some files totaling as much as $758—creating significant cost pressure on the business.

BankSouth Mortgage sought a single, comprehensive verification partner that could integrate directly into both nCino and Encompass—delivering immediate cost savings while improving reporting, simplifying vendor management, and enhancing the borrower experience across its nationwide lending operation.

The Solution

Consumer-Permissioned Verification in nCino & Encompass

Truv partnered closely with BankSouth Mortgage to embed consumer-permissioned verification directly within the two platforms that power their operations: nCino for borrower-facing verifications and Encompass for re-verifications and back-office workflows.

Point of Sale

nCino Integration

Truv powers borrower verifications upfront where approximately 85% of BankSouth Mortgage’s applications originate. Loan officers can request and complete income, employment, and asset verifications without leaving their core nCino workflow. Borrowers receive a seamless experience that guides them through account-linking with clear messaging and minimal friction.

Loan Origination

Encompass Integration

Truv replaced BankSouth Mortgage’s manual re-verification process with on-demand refresh reports directly within Encompass. This eliminates the need for processors to manually chase down re-verification orders, freeing up valuable time and reducing delays as loans approach closing—all at no additional cost.

Truv also delivered enhanced reporting capabilities through a real-time dashboard with performance metrics and analytics—giving BankSouth Mortgage the visibility they lacked with previous providers. Admin-level control over verification workflows enabled the team to customize processes to match their specific operational needs.

The implementation followed a thoughtful, phased approach. Truv worked with BankSouth Mortgage to pilot the solution in select branches first, allowing the team to refine their approach before the company-wide rollout. Internal training materials, branded one-page PDFs, and step-by-step guides ensured loan officers adapted quickly.

Verifications now complete in under 60 seconds—a dramatic improvement over the manual processes that previously slowed down loan processing timelines and frustrated both loan officers and borrowers.
