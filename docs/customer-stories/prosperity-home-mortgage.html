The Challenge

Rising Costs & Low Conversion Rates

Reduce Verification Spending

In 2021, PHM faced rising loan origination costs, primarily driven by legacy verification services, resulting in total verification costs exceeding $492 on a portion of loans.

Enhance Borrower Experience

Improve borrower adoption by simplifying the account linking process and using messaging throughout that builds trust in the process.

Cross-Platform Portability

Integrate with POS while allowing seamless access in LOS without functionality loss or borrower re-engagement.

Security & Compliance

Work with a verification provider that adheres to the highest industry security standards and protocols, including SOC 2 Type II certification, to protect sensitive borrower data and maintain regulatory compliance.

The Solution

Truv: Consumer-Permissioned Verification

PHM selected Truv as its primary VOIE and VOA provider, integrating directly into its Lodasoft point-of-sale system. Implementation focused on borrower education, trust-centered workflows, and data-driven continuous improvements.

Truv provides original paystubs and W2s with every successful verification, enabling PHM to use returned data across 100% of loan files regardless of investor type (agency, non-QM, or others).
