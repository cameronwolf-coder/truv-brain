Known for its commitment to innovation and borrower-first approach, CCM funds over 100,000 loans annually through its expansive network of branches and loan officers across all 50 states.

The Solution

Consumer-Permissioned Verification in Blend & Encompass

Truv partnered closely with CCM to embed consumer-permissioned verification directly within the two platforms that power CCM’s operations: Blend for borrower-facing verifications and Encompass for back-office re-verifications.

Point of Sale

Blend Integration

Truv powers borrower verifications as follow-up tasks, allowing loan officers to request and complete income and employment verifications without ever leaving their core workflow. Borrowers receive a seamless experience that guides them through account-linking with clear messaging and minimal friction.

Loan Origination

Encompass Integration

Truv automates re-verifications—a traditionally manual, time-consuming process—at no additional cost. This eliminates the need for processors to manually chase down re-verification orders, freeing up valuable time and reducing delays as loans approach closing.
