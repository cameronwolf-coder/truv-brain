Bugs Found

Bug 1: SmartLead Router Missing Enterprise Guard FIXED

File: truv-scout/truv_scout/smartlead_router.py:98-102

Previous behavior: Only checked outreach_status before enrollment. Enterprise and government-routed contacts were NOT excluded.

Fix applied: Contacts with lead_routing = enterprise or lead_routing = government are now SKIPPED from SmartLead auto-enrollment.

# In route_to_smartlead(), after outreach_status check:
lead_routing = (result.final_routing or "").lower()
if lead_routing in {"enterprise", "government"}:
    logger.info(f"Skipping {result.contact_id}: lead_routing={lead_routing} (manual outreach)")
    return False

Bug 2: HubSpot Workflow Not Gated on Scout Scoring

System: HubSpot workflow "Order workflow Activation Campaign" (ID: 1688270128)

Current behavior: Fires immediately on contact creation, sends marketing emails before Scout has scored or routed the contact.

Expected behavior: Should either:

  1. Wait for scout_scored_at to be populated before sending, OR
  2. Only target contacts where lead_routing = self-service, OR
  3. Be replaced entirely by the Scout-to-SmartLead pipeline (since SmartLead sequences serve the same purpose)

Impact: Every signup gets duplicate nurture from both HubSpot and SmartLead. Enterprise contacts get automated emails that should be manual SDR touches.

Issue 3: No Suppression Between Product and Marketing Emails

Current behavior: Product emails (finley@mytruvhub.com) and marketing emails (HubSpot workflow + SmartLead) fire independently on the same day.

Expected behavior: Marketing emails should have a 24-48 hour delay after product onboarding emails to avoid overwhelming new signups.
