NEW: MORTGAGE OPS TARGETING LIST (LinkedIn Ads)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Built a precision-targeted contact list for LinkedIn Ads by cross-referencing the Top 250 Mortgage Lenders (2024 HMDA origination data) against HubSpot operations contacts.

How we built it:
  1. Took top 250 mortgage lenders by origination volume (HMDA data)
  2. Removed 39 lenders that are existing Truv customers
  3. Kept 9 deal-stage lenders (UWM, Chase, Wells Fargo, PNC, Citizens, Truist...)
  4. Searched HubSpot for contacts with "operations" in title at remaining 211 lenders
  5. Scored by seniority & selected top 3 VP+ contacts per company

  Target Lenders:      211
  VP+ Contacts:        402
  Companies Covered:   158
  Expected LI Matches: ~160–240
