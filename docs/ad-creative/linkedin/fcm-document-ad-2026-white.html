02 — The Challenge
Background

$2M in Annual Verification Costs & Fragmented Vendor Ecosystem

First Continental Mortgage, Ltd. is a Houston-based builder-focused mortgage lender operating 12 joint venture partnerships and funding approximately 7,000 loans per year. With dual platforms (nCino and Encompass) and construction-cycle re-verifications, FCM faced mounting costs and operational complexity across their verification stack.

nCino & Encompass

Truv deployed across both nCino and Encompass, giving FCM a unified verification solution spanning their entire origination workflow. In nCino, Truv serves as the first line of defense, pulling VOIE and VOA data before loans move downstream. In Encompass, Truv automates pre-closing verifications, eliminating the manual follow-ups that compounded costs during 6-9 month construction cycles.

The phased rollout across all 12 joint ventures included tailored training sessions and a collaborative roadmap with FCM's operations team, ensuring consistent adoption across every entity.
