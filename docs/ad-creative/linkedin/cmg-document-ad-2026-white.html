02 — The Challenge
Background

$1.1M Monthly Verification Spend & Rigid Vendor Architecture

CMG Home Loans (CMG Mortgage, Inc.) is a top 10 nationwide lender funding 50,000+ loans per year across all 50 states and DC, with a proprietary POS (SmartApp) and LO portal (Clear). CMG identified critical challenges with their existing verification model that demanded a modern, borrower-driven approach.

Unsustainable Verification Spend
$1.1M/month on legacy VOIE providers with no path to cost reduction at scale
Rigid Sequential Call Structure
Legacy vendor-ordered model locked CMG into inflexible verification workflows
Limited Integration Capabilities
No ability to embed verification natively into CMG's proprietary tech stack
No Multi-Channel Flexibility
Verification limited to a single channel with no borrower-driven options
Need for Modern Flexible VOIE
Required a borrower-driven strategy delivering a complete income profile across their investor base
“

After assessing the market, it became clear that a traditional, vendor ordered verification model was not enough. We needed a borrower driven strategy that engages the customer directly and delivers a more complete income profile, not just a single point verification.

Paul Akinmade
Chief Strategy Officer, CMG Financial

Truv's solution is fully embedded within CMG's proprietary ecosystem. Borrowers connect their payroll accounts directly through SmartApp, while loan officers manage verifications through the Clear portal with configurable JV partner templates and real-time webhook notifications.

The implementation transformed CMG's rigid sequential call structure into an API-first, borrower-driven model that delivers a more complete income profile without increasing documentation burden or cost.
