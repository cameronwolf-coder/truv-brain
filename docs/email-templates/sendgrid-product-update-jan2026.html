Truv kicked off 2026 with new product enhancements designed to further streamline our client workflows and elevate end-user experiences, delivering expanded functionality and continued improvements across Encompass and Truv Dashboard.

Key Highlights:

  • Encompass Improvements - Consumer Connect®, Enhanced ASO Flexibility
  • Truv Dashboard Updates - Volunteer Letter Customization
  • Truv Bridge & Orders - Add Another Account Feature

Encompass Improvements

  • New Consumer Connect® Integration: We've expanded our ICE Encompass® integration to include Encompass Consumer Connect® Point-of-Sale (POS), enabling borrowers to complete VOI/E/A using Truv upfront in the loan application.
  • Creating Refresh Orders Via ASO: Lenders now have the ability to configure loan-level criteria to trigger a Verification of Employment (VOE) or Deposit-based Verification of Employment (DVOE) refresh automatically prior to closing.
  • ASO Order Notifications Send to Alerts Email: To improve coordination and visibility for automated orders, Truv now sends order status updates to the 'Alerts' email if configured in Truv Dashboard, in addition to the LO, Processor, or any specified default Encompass roles.

Truv Dashboard Updates

  • Customize Volunteer Letter Requirements in Truv Templates: Now, public sector customers have the option to make Volunteer letter uploads mandatory or optional for clients by configuring the template(s) in Truv Dashboard.

Truv Bridge & Orders Updates

  • Add Another Account Feature in Order Landing Page: The "Add another account" button is now supported for orders with predefined employers, providers, or banks, allowing borrowers to add additional accounts in real time as verification requirements evolve.

Customer Spotlight

Other Key Updates:

  • Upload Documents Button in Order Landing Page: Borrowers now have the option to upload docs in fewer steps.
  • Updated Link-Level Reports: Link-level VOIE and VOE reports have been redesigned to align more closely with user-level reports.
