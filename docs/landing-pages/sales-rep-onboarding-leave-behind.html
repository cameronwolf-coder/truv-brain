Know Your Buyer 3

What Resonates with Operations Buyers

Operations leaders care about three things: cost, speed, and scale. They are measured on verification cost per loan, days to close, and team efficiency. Speak their language.

Mortgage Operations Executive Primary Buyer

COO, SVP of Operations, VP of Operations, VP of Lending - Reports to CEO/President
Pain #1: Cost "Verification costs are out of control" - $40K-$200K/year, 40% database miss rate means paying twice
Pain #2: Speed "Manual VOE killing close times" - 5-7 days per verification, constant borrower follow-up
Pain #3: Risk "Buyback risk keeps me up at night" - $50K-$500K+ per buyback, GSE getting stricter
Pain #4: Scale "Can't scale without hiring" - Every loan requires the same manual effort

How To Open With Ops Buyers

Lead with cost savings and scale. They respond to specific dollar amounts, not percentages. Calculate their potential savings before the call:

  • Know their volume: "[Company] originated X loans last quarter..."
  • Lead with the math: "At $350 savings per loan, that's $X annually..."
  • Name the pain: "What do you do for the 40% TWN doesn't cover?"
  • Drop a name: "CrossCountry is saving $10M annually. AmeriSave cut costs 80%."

How to Talk About Truv 6

Sound Like a Peer, Not a Vendor

Truv sounds like a senior lending ops practitioner who's been in the trenches. Confident expert, not corporate vendor. Lead with specifics, not buzzwords.

Do Say

  • "CrossCountry saved $10M annually."
  • "What do you do for the 40% TWN misses?"
  • "96% coverage, 30 seconds, $350 per loan."
  • "Most lenders at your scale face buyback risk from stale verification data."
  • "Worth 15 minutes to discuss?"
  • "Save up to 80%"

Never Say

  • "We're revolutionizing the verification space"
  • "I'd love to learn more about your needs!"
  • "Our AI-powered, seamless platform"
  • "Best-in-class, world-class, cutting-edge"
  • "Leverage our solution to optimize your workflow"
  • "Our customers see incredible ROI"

Banned Words

Revolutionary, game-changing, disruptive, AI-powered, all-in-one, best-in-class, world-class, cutting-edge, leverage, utilize, synergy, seamless, "I'd love to...", "looking forward to partnering"
