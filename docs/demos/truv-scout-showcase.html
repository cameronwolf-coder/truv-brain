05
SCORE COMPOSITION

What goes into the number.

Form Fit 35%

Title seniority, use case match, loan/app volume, company size

Deal Context 20%

Existing deals, lifecycle stage, sales activity, target account flag

External Triggers 20%

Apollo tech stack matches (LOS/POS, VOI/VOE), hiring signals, revenue intel

Engagement 15%

Email opens/clicks, site visits, recency of last interaction

Timing 10%

Days since last activity, first touch recency, form submission freshness

06
ROUTING ENGINE

Right lead, right lane.

🏢
Enterprise Exec/VP/Director title OR 10k+ apps/yr OR 3k+ loans/yr OR Apollo: 100+ employees / $50M+ rev
🏛️
Government Use case = "Public Services" or government-related keywords detected
🧑‍💻

🚫
Not a Lead "Log In" or "Verification Help" form submissions — filtered out automatically
