Initiative 5
Critical Low Effort Week 1

Domain Infrastructure Audit

Deliverability failures account for 3 of the top 9 outbound failure reasons. This is table stakes before scaling anything else.

Deliverability Rules
  • NEVER send cold from primary domain (truv.com)
  • Max 25 emails/day per mailbox
  • Max 5-10 mailboxes per domain
  • Pre-age domains 2-4 weeks
  • Open tracking OFF (pixel hurts deliverability)
  • No images or attachments in cold emails
