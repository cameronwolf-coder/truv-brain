## Writing Style Observations

### Headlines
- Lead with company name + quantified result
- Format: "[Company] [Action Verb] [Metric] with Truv"
- Examples:
  - "CrossCountry Mortgage reduces verification spend by $10M annually"
  - "AFCU Achieves 65% Conversion and Saves 80%"
  - "MIG Improves Conversion by 100%"

### Metrics Presentation
- Large hero numbers at top (80%, $10M, 65%)
- 3-4 key metrics per story
- Specific numbers preferred over ranges
- Always include % or $ symbols

### Quote Attribution
- Format: "Quote text" — Name, Title, Company
- 3-5 quotes per long-form story
- Quotes drive narrative forward
- Focus on partnership and results

### Section Structure
1. Hero metrics
2. About the company
3. Problem/Challenge
4. Truv Solution
5. The Story (quote-driven narrative)
6. Results/Outcomes
7. CTA
