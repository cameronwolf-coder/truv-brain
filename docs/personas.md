# Buyer Personas

## Mortgage Operations Executive

### Profile
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Titles** | COO, SVP of Operations, VP of Operations, VP of Lending |
| **Industry** | Mortgage Lending |
| **Reports to** | CEO, President |
| **Team size** | 20-200+ (ops, processing, underwriting) |

### Pain Points (In Their Words)

| Pain Point | Details |
|------------|---------|
| "Verification costs out of control" | $40K-$200K/year spend, 40% database miss rate |
| "Manual VOE killing close times" | 5-7 days, constant borrower follow-up |
| "Buyback risk keeps me up at night" | $50K-$500K+ per buyback, GSE getting stricter |
| "Can't scale without hiring" | Every loan = same manual effort |
| "Fraud everywhere" | Fake pay stubs, photoshopped statements |

### Success Metrics
- Verification cost per loan
- Days to close (18 to 11 days target)
- Buyback rate / rep & warranty risk
- Pull-through rate
- Ops team efficiency

### Messaging Angle
Lead with cost savings and scale. They care about operational efficiency and reducing risk. Use language like "eliminate manual effort" and "zero buybacks."
