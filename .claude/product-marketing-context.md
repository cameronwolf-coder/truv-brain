# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-02-24*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Truv is the consumer-permissioned data platform that verifies income, employment, and assets in real-time — at up to 80% lower cost than instant databases.
**What it does:** Truv connects directly to 45+ payroll providers and 13,000+ financial institutions so lenders can verify borrower data in 30 seconds instead of 5-7 days. The platform replaces expensive instant databases (The Work Number) and manual verification processes with direct-source, real-time data that flows straight into existing loan systems like Encompass.
**Product category:** Consumer-permissioned data / verification platform
**Product type:** B2B SaaS (API + SDK + no-code dashboard)
**Business model:** Per-verification pricing across three tiers:
- **Launch** — Build and test (unlimited test credentials, onboarding support)
- **Professional** — 35%+ savings guaranteed (dedicated AM, SOC2 access, Slack/Teams support)
- **Enterprise** — 70%+ savings guaranteed (white-glove support, dedicated SE, SLAs, SSO, QBRs)

## Problems & Pain Points

**Core problem:** Lenders pay too much and wait too long for borrower verification data — and the data they get is often stale, incomplete, or missing entirely.

**Why alternatives fall short:**
- **The Work Number (Equifax):** Covers only ~60% of US workforce (40% miss rate), costs up to $123/verification with ~20% price increases every 6 months, data can be weeks old, charges full price for re-verifications, requires manual re-keying
- **Manual VOE:** Takes 5-7 days, 60% completion rate, constant borrower follow-up for documents, processor time spent on data entry
- **Other consumer-permissioned providers (Plaid/Finicity/Argyle):** Lower conversion rates, weaker LOS integrations, no free re-verifications
- **Building in-house:** 12-18 months, $500K+ development cost, ongoing maintenance

**What it costs them:**
- $40K-$200K/year in verification spend (mortgage)
- 3-7 extra days per loan from manual processes
- $50K-$500K+ per GSE buyback from stale/bad data
- 15-25 processor-minutes per file on verification data entry
- 40% of loans require manual fallback when database misses

**Emotional tension:**
- "Verification costs out of control"
- "Manual VOE killing close times"
- "Buyback risk keeps me up at night"
- "Can't scale without hiring"
- "Fraud everywhere" — fake pay stubs, photoshopped statements

## Competitive Landscape

**Direct:** The Work Number (Equifax Workforce Solutions) — 60% of mortgage prospects use them. Falls short because: only 60% workforce coverage, up to $123/verification with 20% price hikes every 6 months, data can be weeks stale, charges full price for re-verifications, requires manual re-keying into LOS.

**Secondary:** Manual VOE process — 25% of prospects. Falls short because: 5-7 days per verification, 60% completion rate, constant borrower follow-up, processor re-keying, stale data by closing.

**Indirect:** Other consumer-permissioned providers (Plaid, Finicity, Argyle) — 15% of prospects. Falls short because: lower conversion rates, weaker Encompass integration, no free re-verifications, less granular data. Truv's angle here is additive (complement), not replacement.

**Build in-house:** Falls short because: 12-18 months timeline, $500K+ cost, ongoing maintenance burden, no GSE approvals.

## Differentiation

**Key differentiators:**
- 96% US workforce coverage vs. 60% for The Work Number
- Up to 80% lower cost with transparent, consistent pricing
- Real-time direct-source data (not a stale database)
- Free re-verifications within 120 days
- Native Encompass integration (auto-populates, no re-keying)
- GSE-approved: Fannie Mae Day 1 Certainty + Freddie Mac AIM
- Waterfall verification (payroll → bank income → document upload) for 100% borrower coverage
- 99.99% fraud detection rate
- 65-70%+ post-login VOIE conversion rates

**How we do it differently:** Consumer-permissioned, direct-to-source verification. The borrower connects their payroll account (30 seconds), and Truv pulls verified data in real-time from the employer's payroll system — not from a purchased database. This means the data is always current, always complete, and always permissioned.

**Why that's better:** Fresher data reduces buyback risk. Direct-source eliminates the 40% miss rate. Consumer-permissioned costs a fraction of instant databases. And the borrower experience is 30 seconds vs. hunting for pay stubs.

**Why customers choose us:**
- Cost savings that are immediately measurable ($350+/loan)
- Partnership approach — "Truv has redefined what partnership means" (CrossCountry)
- Hands-on support (4-hour response times vs. "couldn't reach a human for 2 years")
- Speed to go live (days-to-weeks, not months)
- Data quality — "more granular, comprehensive, broken down by income source" (MortgageRight)

**Pull (what attracts them to Truv):**

**How we address each anxiety:**
