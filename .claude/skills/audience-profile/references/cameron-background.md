## Writing Voice (Summary)

- Conversational authority: expertise through data, not credentials
- Thinking in public: shows reasoning, not polished conclusions
- Punchy and direct: short sentences, fragments
- Raw honesty: no sanitizing
- Vulnerability without victimhood
- Humor as pressure release
- No em-dashes (ever), no "Here's the thing," no motivational speaker energy
- Start in the middle, max 3 main points, end with "for now" energy
